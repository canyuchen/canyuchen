### 👋 About Me

Hi, this is **Canyu Chen (陈灿宇)**. ### 🔬 Research

My research interest covers **Foundation Agent, Trustworthiness, and Multimodality**. I aim to pursue **Safe and Aligned Artificial General Intelligence** in the long run.

- 🤖 **[FedAgent](https://drive.google.com/file/d/16cZQ-26NXLsy1ZkrJNfWNYSyI5jLHfru/view)** enables agent learning without sacrificing user data privacy &nbsp; (first author · 🏆 [Best Paper Award](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1S13-8c382w8urNDCLwli1PNdc3ABnW1H/view?usp=sharing) at the [AAAI'26 TrustAgent Workshop](https://trustagenticai.github.io/AAAI2026/) · 🏆 [Outstanding Paper Award](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1Z1x_cvT2I6aFdsF0ai25ZksLRcQ_VLMo/view?usp=sharing) at the [AAAI'26 PerFM Workshop](https://personalizedllm.github.io/events/perfm-aaai26/))
- 🤝 **[AgentTrust](https://agent-trust.camel-ai.org/)** demonstrates the feasibility to simulate human trust behavior with LLM agents &nbsp; (co-first author · 🏆 [Outstanding Paper Award](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1dHwovA2ylmzamqAhjUpGSWS3yhhhqLXm/view?usp=sharing) at the [CIKM'25 LASS Workshop](https://lass-workshop.github.io/))
- 📰 **[LLMFake](https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.13788)** shows that LLM-generated misinformation can be more deceptive than human-written misinformation &nbsp; (first author · 🏆 [Didactic Paper Award](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1oHsW2q1yEQgQ5GfBtO_nrER75053eBIW/view?usp=sharing) at the [NeurIPS'23 ICBINB Workshop](https://sites.google.com/view/icbinb-2023))

### 🌐 Communities & Initiatives

- I am one organizer of the **[ResponsibleFM](https://responsible-fm.github.io/)** community, dedicated to advancing socially responsible and trustworthy foundation models (language and multimodal).
- I started and led the **[LLMs Meet Misinformation](https://llm-misinformation.github.io/)** initiative, aiming to combat misinformation in the age of LLMs.
